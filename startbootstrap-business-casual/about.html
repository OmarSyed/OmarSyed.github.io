Who I Am

I am originally from Karachi, Pakistan. My family moved to the Yonkers, New York when I was two. I graduated from Yonkers High School in 2015 before attending Stony Brook University.

I am an avid reader of both fantasy and nonfiction with one of my favorite series of all time being A Song of Ice and Fire by George RR Martin. One of my favorite biographies is Einstein: His Life and Universe by Walter Isaacson. I also enjoy playing video games, especially real time strategy and empire-builder games such as Total War and Mount and Blade: Warband.


My favorite sport to play and watch is basketball. My favorite team is the Chicago Bulls, however, I am currently rooting for the Houston Rockets to win the NBA championship.

Why Computer Science?

Although I was initially planning on pursuing medicine, college opened my eyes to the extravagant realm of technology. When I took my first computer science course, I became intrigued by its complexity and mode of thinking. I found myself enjoying programming and delving deeper. I realized then that this fascination is nothing new - it's been there since the age of 4 when I got my first desktop computer.
